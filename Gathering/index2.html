About The Gathering

The Gathering is a powerful evening of Jewish women’s unity, connection, and inspiration. Join women from across Washington State for a mega challah bake where hundreds will knead, braid, and bless together, followed by an uplifting concert with renowned singer Devorah Schwartz. It’s a night to nourish the soul, bond through tradition, and celebrate the strength and spirit of Jewish sisterhood.

Evening highlights

Mega Challah Bake

Experience the magic of hundreds of women coming together to mix, knead, and shape challah in unison. Guided with meaning and tradition, the challah bake is more than a culinary experience—it’s a moment of connection, prayer, and shared purpose. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or it’s your first time, you’ll leave inspired, uplifted, and with fresh challahs ready to bring light and blessing into your home.

Live Concert with Devorah Schwartz

Devorah Schwartz is a singer and songwriter whose soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics resonate deeply with women of all backgrounds. With a unique blend of timeless technique and contemporary sound, her music explores themes of healing, growth, and connection. Known for making every listener feel seen, Devorah brings a powerful, emotional presence to the stage—promising an unforgettable concert that will uplift, inspire, and leave you feeling spiritually renewed.

Partner With The Gathering
